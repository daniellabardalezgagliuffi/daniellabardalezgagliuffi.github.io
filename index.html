About me

I am a Kalbfleisch Postdoctoral Fellow at the American Museum of Natural History in New York City. I want to understand the physical processes leading to the formation and evolution of the lowest mass stars, brown dwarfs and most massive planets. I analyze observational data to contrain statistics on the local population of low mass stars and brown dwarfs, and want to apply these skills and knowledge to giant exoplanets. I am interested in understading the evolution of cool atmospheres across the range of masses encompassing low mass stars, brown dwarfs and giant planets.
